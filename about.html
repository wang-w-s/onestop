Our Mission
College is stressful enough without having to scour the internet for all the tools and information needed to even get started. We aim to provide One-Stop for students. Hover over the image below for more information!
Music
One-Stop provides relaxing music to improve the quality of time spent studying, without the inconvenience of having multiple apps/websites to switch between.
Organization
We understand that you have to keep up with a multitude of different tasks on a daily basis, and staying organized is the only way to succeed. Our customizable calendar is designed to keep you on track.
Rest
Just as important to your success as good study habits, your mental and physical health should be a priority. Maintain your performance, and your happiness with our self-care guide.
Tips
Access our list of helpful study tips and tricks to give you a leg up on your upcoming exams.
